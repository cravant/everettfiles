EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Operation EVERETT represents one of the most significant counterintelligence investigations undertaken by the Joseph Coolidge Agency in the past decade. The case centers on Dr. Michael James Everett, a former NSA contractor with access to highly classified signals intelligence programs, who is suspected of unauthorized data exfiltration, espionage, and potential collaboration with foreign intelligence services.

Initial detection occurred on 03 March 2025 when NSA security systems flagged anomalous network activity originating from Everett's workstation at Fort Meade. Subsequent forensic analysis revealed systematic unauthorized access to classified databases, encrypted file transfers to external servers, and communications with individuals later identified as foreign intelligence operatives.

The scope and sensitivity of potentially compromised information necessitates continued investigation with maximum operational security. This dossier contains all available intelligence, surveillance evidence, and recovered communications related to Subject EVERETT.

BACKGROUND

Michael Everett was born in Columbus, Ohio, to middle-class parents with no known connections to foreign governments or intelligence services. He demonstrated exceptional aptitude in mathematics and computer science from an early age, graduating summa cum laude from MIT in 2006 with degrees in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science.

Following graduate studies at Stanford University, where he earned a Ph.D. in Cryptography in 2011, Everett was recruited directly into the intelligence community. He initially worked as a civilian employee at NSA's Cryptanalysis and Exploitation Services division before transitioning to contractor status with Quantum Defense Solutions in 2019.

Throughout his career, Everett maintained TS/SCI clearance and access to some of the nation's most sensitive signals intelligence programs. His work focused on developing algorithms for breaking encrypted communications and analyzing foreign cryptographic systems. Performance reviews consistently rated him as exceptional, with no prior security incidents or concerns.

INTELLIGENCE ASSESSMENT

Based on recovered evidence and ongoing investigation, JCA assesses with high confidence that Dr. Michael Everett engaged in systematic espionage activity over a period of at least two years. The sophistication of his operational tradecraft suggests he received training from professional intelligence operatives.

The volume and sensitivity of compromised material represents a significant national security breach. Preliminary damage assessment indicates potential exposure of sources and methods across multiple intelligence compartments. Full scope of compromise may not be determinable for months or years.
