Operational Divisions

Organizational Structure — February 2026

Intelligence Division

DIV-01 | INTEL

The Intelligence Division serves as JCA's analytical core, responsible for processing raw intelligence, producing threat assessments, and providing strategic guidance to operational units. The division maintains direct liaison with the intelligence community, including NSA, CIA, DIA, and allied foreign services.

Primary Functions

  • All-source intelligence analysis and production
  • Counterintelligence threat assessment
  • Strategic warning and indicators monitoring
  • Intelligence community coordination
  • Foreign intelligence collection management
247 Analysts
18 Liaison Officers
SCIF Secure Facility

Cyber Operations Division

DIV-02 | CYBER

Cyber Operations Division conducts digital forensics, incident response, and offensive cyber operations against adversaries targeting U.S. networks. The division operates the National Cyber Investigative Joint Task Force and maintains partnerships with private sector cybersecurity firms to protect critical infrastructure.

Primary Functions

  • Cyber threat hunting and attribution
  • Network defense and incident response
  • Digital forensics and malware analysis
  • Critical infrastructure protection
  • Offensive cyber operations (authorized)
312 Cyber Specialists
24/7 Operations Center
9,400 Incidents/Year

Field Operations Division

DIV-03 | FIELDOPS

Field Operations coordinates all investigative activities conducted outside headquarters, managing eighty-nine field offices and specialized tactical units. The division deploys special agents, surveillance teams, and tactical operators in support of investigations requiring physical presence and direct action.

Primary Functions

  • Criminal and counterintelligence investigations
  • Surveillance and technical operations
  • Tactical response and arrest operations
  • Witness protection and asset management
  • International liaison and coordination
4,200 Field Agents
89 Field Offices
32 Foreign Posts

Analysis & Research Division

DIV-04 | A&R

The Analysis & Research Division develops investigative methodologies, conducts behavioral analysis, and provides technical support to ongoing operations. The division houses the Behavioral Analysis Unit, the Laboratory Division, and advanced research programs exploring emerging technologies and tradecraft.

Primary Functions

  • Behavioral profiling and threat assessment
  • Forensic science and laboratory analysis
  • Research and development of investigative tools
  • Training and professional development
  • Biometric and identity intelligence
189 Scientists
45 Behavioral Analysts
ISO Certified Labs

Counterterrorism Division

DIV-05 | CT

Counterterrorism Division leads JCA's efforts to identify, disrupt, and dismantle terrorist organizations threatening the United States. Working closely with the Department of Homeland Security and military special operations forces, the division tracks financing networks, recruitment operations, and plots in development.

Primary Functions

  • Terrorism threat tracking and disruption
  • Financial intelligence and sanctions enforcement
  • Radicalization monitoring and intervention
  • Joint Terrorism Task Force coordination
  • International counterterrorism partnerships
421 CT Specialists
67 JTTF Units
TOP Priority Level

Counterintelligence Division

DIV-06 | CI

The Counterintelligence Division protects U.S. national security by detecting, identifying, and neutralizing foreign intelligence operations. The division conducts investigations of espionage, manages double agent operations, and provides security briefings to government personnel with access to classified information.

Primary Functions

  • Espionage investigation and prosecution
  • Foreign intelligence service monitoring
  • Insider threat detection and mitigation
  • Security clearance adjudication support
  • Economic espionage and trade secret theft
198 CI Officers
HUMINT Primary Method
